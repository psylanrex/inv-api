# About Us
## Primary Innovations:
A single, shared, immersive, persistent, 3D virtual space where humans experience life in ways they could not in the physical world.

## Differentiation
The above definition implies that the metaverse will have a single source, but we do not believe this to be the case.  Rather, we see it as decentralized with portability, the ability to teleport, between different regions.  Developers will create unique experiences that users from across the metaverse can enjoy.
